Welcome to MeloBloom!

A website designed to help soothe your nerves and destress after a long day of work. Boss annoying you at work? Your partner complaining about your lack of finance? Groupmates complaining about the lack of work done? Well look no further! MeloBloom can solve all of your problems. Designed by Solomon Powell, Aqil Rahim, and Nelson Suarez, MeloBloom offers music catered to your emotions and features our personal rhythm game, Rect (D, A, S, H), to help you cool down after a stressful day. If you want to know more about the logic behind our website, feel free to go check the Our Goals section! Have fun!
