Why MeloBloom?

It has been proven that music can act as a method of relief and is a catalyst to help soothe your nerves and let you wind down. Studies found that 75% of people feel less stressed when listening to their favorite song, and 50% of people are more productive whilst listening to music. The effects music has on the mind and body are positive, and as depicted in an experiment by a group of Finnish researchers. Patients with music therapy had a lower depression score after 6 months than in psychotherapy alone!

Our Vision

As the popularity for music therapy as a stress reliever continues to rise, our team is committed to creating a website dedicated to helping you feel better; or worse, whatever floats your boat! Combining the serotonin boost you get from listening to music and the thrills and joys of playing video games, MeloBloom is designed to help you relieve stress in whichever way you want! Feeling sad and want to listen to some happy tunes to cheer you up? MeloBloom can do that. Feeling angry but you want some angry beats to fuel your rage? MeloBloom can help with that! Currently a work in progress, MeloBloom will continue expanding it's reservoir of music in order to better suit your tastes!
